About Mark Whisenant

The Man, the Myth, the Legend

Posted by Mark Whisenant

Mark Haynes Whisenant was born on a snowy winter day in February of 2003. As a child, he was curious, energetic, and well spoken for his age. He grew up in a loving family, with his parents and sister, and eventually a dog. He values his family above all else and considers his education to be one of the most important things. Mark hopes that he can be a role model for others, and to be kind to everyone he meets.

Mark loves music, with a very broad range of music he enjoys. He has a famous playlist on spotify, which you can view here

Mark enjoys spending time with his family, friends, and his girlfriend. He loves his dog and hopes that she lives forever.
